The Proposal

Melissa and Kevin got engaged in New Orleans at the end of an all-day scavenger hunt that Kevin made for Melissa that had them eating, drinking, and solving clues throughout the city.

Since the two have a history of making scavenger hunt puzzles for each other (so nerdy, I know), Melissa followed the clues of what she thought was her Christmas present, not knowing what waited for her at the end.

From a voodoo shop fortune teller, to a spinning carousel bar, to a Venetian gondola ride, Melissa solved the clues in the app Kevin made for her, until the final twist: an engagement ring pulled out of AR (augmented reality) ring pops!
